About Me

A passionate innovator bridging GIS technology with business strategy, one solution at a time.

I’m Abhik Naskar, a geospatial expert and business development professional currently based in Bangalore. My journey began with a fascination for maps and spatial patterns during my Geography undergraduate days at the University of Calcutta, which evolved into a deep technical expertise in Geoinformatics from Symbiosis Institute.

What drives me is the thrill of innovation—whether it’s developing cutting-edge flood mapping solutions using Google Earth Engine, automating GIS workflows with Python, or designing full-stack web mapping applications. I love solving complex problems by blending technology with creative thinking.

At AGSRT, I wear multiple hats: from conducting client visits and generating leads to architecting geospatial solutions and managing ERP systems. My experience spans across NielsenIQ where I created PSUs/SSUs for multiple countries, and Xpressbees where I worked on address parsing and spatial databases.

Beyond the professional realm, I’m deeply passionate about music production, cooking, travel, and writing. I believe in continuous learning and finding joy in both code and creativity. Whether I’m composing beats in FL Studio or experimenting with new recipes, I approach everything with the same enthusiasm I bring to solving geospatial challenges.
